FAQ

Does Synapse store student data?

Student records live in your Firebase project under your control. We never expose your AI keys.

Can I use my own standards?

Yes. Drop JSON chunks into /standards/ and they appear in the picker.

Will this work on school devices?

Yes—no extensions needed. Works on modern browsers with your district Google account.
